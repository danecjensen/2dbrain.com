Camping box

April 2013

The camping box is simple. It's a box you keep ready to go camping at any moment. When you decide to "go get out in it" you won't be thinking about what you need. You'll be ready to enjoy nature.

Here are sone guiding principles to keep your camping box ready:

  • If it's useful for camping and fits in the box (not tent, not sleeping bag, etc) or actual food/drink, add it to the box.
  • IF YOU TAKE SOMETHING OUT OF THE BOX PUT IT BACK. You might need to use something while at home and you are going to jam yourself if you don't add it back.

non-food box:

  • lantern
  • fuel
  • knife
  • bug spray
  • sunblock
  • first aid kit
  • frisbees
  • pens
  • paper
  • toilet paper
  • gloves
  • deck of cards
  • cards against humanity
